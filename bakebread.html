Curriculum Breakdown

Introduction to Bread Baking

  • Kitchen equipment, safety, and hygiene
  • Bread history, types, and science
  • Yeast fermentation and dough chemistry
  • Importance of gluten, proofing, and kneading
  • Introduction to flour types and hydration
  • Bread baking terminologies
  • HACCP & food safety practices

Foundational Breads

  • White sandwich bread
  • Dinner rolls & pull-apart rolls
  • Whole wheat & multigrain loaves
  • Banana bread, coconut bread
  • Sardine bread, coconut milk bread
  • Chocolate babka, sugar swirl bread
  • Cinnamon rolls

Intermediate Doughs & Enriched Breads

  • Brioche, milk bread, and challah
  • Sourdough starter creation and maintenance
  • Focaccia (classic and stuffed)
  • Bagels and pretzels
  • Pain de mie and dinner knots
  • Stuffed breads (cheese, spinach, garlic)
  • Vegan and gluten-free breads

Artisan Bread Making

  • French baguettes
  • Sourdough batards and boules
  • Ciabatta
  • Rye bread & rustic loaves
  • No-knead Dutch oven baking
  • Cold fermentation and autolyse method
  • Poolish, Biga, and Levain techniques

International Breads

  • Indian naan and paratha
  • Middle Eastern pita and khubz
  • African agege bread & coconut rolls
  • Caribbean hard dough bread
  • Turkish simit
  • Italian focaccia & grissini
  • Chinese steamed buns (mantou, bao)

Bread Accompaniments & Spreads

  • Garlic butter and herb oils
  • Hummus, tzatziki, baba ganoush
  • Homemade jams & custards
  • Compound butters (cinnamon, vanilla, honey)
  • Cheese spreads, sandwich sauces

Bread-based Street Foods & Fusion Baking

  • Bread pizza & toasties
  • Stuffed buns (meat, cheese, veggies)
  • Bread rolls for sliders
  • Sandwich development class
  • Canapés using artisan bases
  • Cultural fusion bread recipes

Bread Art & Presentation

  • Shaping and scoring techniques
  • Decorative rolls and braids
  • The art of proofing baskets
  • Oven steam control and crust development
  • Bread display and photography tips

Menu Planning & Bakery Operations

  • Recipe costing and ingredient budgeting
  • Menu curation for bakeries and cafes
  • Inventory tracking and bulk ordering
  • Production timelines and baking schedules
  • Packaging, shelf-life, and storage

Business & Certification

  • Starting a home bakery or micro-bakery
  • Branding, marketing, and pricing strategy
  • Customer service & delivery systems
  • Internship placements for top students
  • Certificate of Completion in Bread Baking Mastery

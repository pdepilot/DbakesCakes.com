Cake Baking and Decoration Course
(Confectionery Art Mastery)

"This short course empowers participants with essential cake baking and decorating skills, blending hands-on experience with creative techniques for a dynamic and practical learning journey."

For cake baking and decoration classes, students are required to bring their own choice of ingredients and decoration supplies they wish to work with.

Practical Hands-On Experience

Module 1: Introduction to Baking

  • Overview of Essential Baking Tools and Equipment
  • Understanding Ingredients and Their Roles
  • Basic Baking Techniques

Module 2: Cake Foundations

  • Exploring Different Cake Types (Sponge, Butter, Cup cakes etc.)
  • Perfecting Cake Batter Mixing and Baking
  • Troubleshooting Common Cake Issues

Module 3: Frosting Fundamentals

  • Various Types of Frostings and Icings
  • Achieving Smooth and Flawless Cake Frosting
  • Creative Frosting Techniques

Module 4: Decoration Delights

  • Introduction to Cake Decorating Tools
  • Basic Piping Techniques
  • Edible Decorations: Fondant, Gum Paste, and Chocolate

Module 5: Theme-Based Cake Designs

  • Designing Cakes for Different Occasions (Birthdays, Weddings, etc.)
  • Creating Tiered Cakes
  • Incorporating Colors and Theme

Module 7: Equipment Usage

  • Gain proficiency in using common baking tools and equipment

Module 8: Time Management

  • Develop skills for efficient time management in a baking setting

Module 9: Presentation Skills

  • Explore basic principles of presenting baked goods attractively

Module 10: Business Basics (Optional)

  • Basics of Starting a Home-Based Cake Decorating Business
  • Pricing Strategies and Marketing Your Creations

Practical Sessions:

  • Hands-on Cake Baking and Decorating Sessions
  • Project Work: Design and Decorate Your Own Cake
  • Project Work: Design and Decorate wedding, Birthdays Cakes for clients

Assessment:

  • Practical Evaluation of Baked and Decorated Cakes
  • Written Test on Baking Techniques and Principles
